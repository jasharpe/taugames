Tauggle

Get free for iOS » Get free for Android »

Tauggle is a word search game, where you try to find words that follow twisting paths through a randomly generated board of letters.

I've always loved this style of word game, but have been frustrated by several flaws as a solitaire app, and have aimed to fix these problems.

I’ve personally read the entire dictionary, making sure that every word in the dictionary is fairly common. I aim for you to never get stuck by a word you don’t know!

A progressive (optional) hint system helps you find all the words on the board, making this one of the only apps of its kind where getting 100% of the words is possible!

Game settings are flexible, allowing 3x3, 4x4, 5x5, 6x6 boards, and 3, 4, or 5 minimum letters per word. Tauggle also features an optional “Reuse Tiles” unique game mode, which allows you to use the same tile multiple times per word, unlocking a new and challenging puzzle!
