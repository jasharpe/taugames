INCOMING! (note - name subject to betterment.)
# Players: 2
Deck: all blue suits, all hearts, black joker (69 cards)
Hearts are trump.
Aces low, face cards go J Q K B C P G.
Black Joker reverses the rank, so G of hearts is the lowest card instead of the highest. Trump becomes the first blue suit played. **only for the trick(s) in which the joker is in play**
Deal: one 9-card pile to each player, top card face up, one 10-card hand to each player, secret
Play: Non-dealer leads first trick, after which the trick-winner leads. If no one wins the trick, the previous leader leads again. 
4-card tricks, played alternately.
The face-up cards on the piles ARE IN PLAY, with some special rules. If a face-up card is the winning card for a trick, no one wins that trick.
After each trick, each player picks up their face-up card and turns up the next in the pile.
Play until the hands run out.
Score: points / trick won.
Play until a set score or set number of hands.
